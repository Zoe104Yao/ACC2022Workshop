Program

 

The workshop will be held in-person on June 7 (Tuesday), 2022.

The workshop includes 12 talks from invited speakers, 5-min contributed talks, poster sessions, and panel discussions. We will accept extended abstracts (maximum two pages) of work-in-progress or recently published results.

We will send out call for submission in March 2022 through various mailing lists, and the abstracts will be due in April 2022. Each accepted abstract will be given a 5-min spotlight presentation and will be invited to present their posters during lunch and coffee breaks.

All times are listed in Eastern Standard Time (EST), UTC -5
